Welcome to Online Crime Information System

Welcome to the official crime information webpage for Lucknow, your one-stop destination for staying informed and engaged in matters of public safety. Our mission is to empower citizens with accurate, up-to-date crime data and resources to promote a safer and more secure community. Real-time Crime Updates: Access real-time crime alerts and notifications directly from our webpage, keeping you informed about incidents in your vicinity. Interactive Crime Maps: Visualize crime patterns and trends across Lucknow using our interactive crime maps. Identify hotspots and areas of concern to make informed decisions about your safety.Online Crime Reporting: Conveniently report crimes directly from our webpage. Anonymous Crime Tips: Share valuable information about crimes anonymously, helping us prevent and investigate criminal activities effectively.
